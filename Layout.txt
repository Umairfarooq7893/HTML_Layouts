=>Explanation of Each Tag
    Tag	        Purpose
    <main>	    Contains the primary content of the webpage. There should
                usually be only one <main> element per page.

    <section>	Groups related content together, often with a heading.

    <article>	Represents a self-contained piece of content
                such as a blog post, news article, or forum post.

    <aside>	    Contains content related to the main content but not essential to it,
                such as sidebars, advertisements, or related links.
